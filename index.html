Meet BoozeHound

The night life app that will give you local happy hour deals based on your location.

  • Login with Social Media

Find out more

Login with Social Media

With the BoozHound app, you can log into the application using either your Facebook or Google +
If you don't have either a Facebook or Google + account, you can use the app too.

Save your favorite venues for quick access later

Find Happy Hour Deals Around You

With BoozHound, all local venues and happy hour deals are displayed on the homepage.
The list of venues and happy hour deals are dependent on your general location

"With BoozeHound, I can find great places and prices anywhere in the world!"

Dylan Chavez Huntsville, TX

Search for Deals

With Google Maps API, any user can look on the map and find local venues.


You have the option to search for any location within the map and can find other venues and their happy hour deals.

"BoozeHound allows me and my girlfriends to find the best local bars and happy hour deals easily and effectively!""

Kelly Ryan London, TX

Find the Right Place

Filter venue results by drink type, hours open, location proximity, and price.


Once the choices have been narrowed down, the right venue is displayed and you can then call the venue, get directions to the place, save the venue by favoriting it, or sharing it on social media.
